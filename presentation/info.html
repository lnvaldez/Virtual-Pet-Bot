TUTORIAL

Activating the Virtual Pet Bot

  • Click on the provided link to add the Virtual Pet Bot to your Discord server.
  • Ensure you have the necessary permissions to add bots to the server.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to authorize the bot and add it to your server.

Your Virtual Pet

  • Once the bot is added to your server, open the channel where you want to interact with your virtual pet.
  • Type the command !create_pet [pet_name] [pet_type], replacing [pet_name] with the name you want to give your pet and [pet_type] with the type of pet you desire (e.g., penguin, squirrel, goldfish, cat).
  • Hit Enter, and the bot will create your virtual pet.

Interacting with Your Virtual Pet

  • Once your virtual pet is created, you can interact with it using buttons:
  • Feed: Click on the "Feed" button to feed your pet and decrease its hunger while increasing happiness and energy.
  • Play: Click on the "Play" button to play with your pet, increasing its hunger slightly but boosting happiness.
  • Talk: Engage in conversation with your pet using the "Talk" button, powered by the Ollama AI, to provide some interactive fun.
  • Status: Check your pet's status by clicking on the "Status" button, which will display its current hunger, happiness, and energy levels.
  • Sleep: Click on the "Sleep" button to allow your pet to rest and replenish its energy while slightly increasing hunger.

Enjoying Your Virtual Pet

Engage with your virtual pet regularly to keep it happy, healthy, and entertained! Experiment with different interactions and watch as your pet grows and thrives in your Discord server. Have fun!
